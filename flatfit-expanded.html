Additional Work

Along with the actual product, a young start-up needs users and investors. To develop interest in the app, I developed a series of marketing strategies of creative content. One series called “Find a Roommate Who…” invoked a series of eye-catching animations and illustrations to appeal to the primary persona. Additionally, ads were created in both English and Spanish to appeal to the international crowd of expats the app was hoping to attract. Beyond an online marketing campaign, we needed a ‘one-page’ to pass out at conventions and networking events to attract potential investors. I worked based off a content framework to design this graphic-heavy marketing collateral.

Photography.

At the University of South Carolina, Visual Communications majors are required to take two semesters of photography classes to understand how to operate a manual Canon 40D DSLR for both advertising and editorial use. Additionally, students learn to use a Canon 80D to shoot videography.

I used the skills I accquired in this class to save my current company, Government Executive, money on freelance photographers. For our product Leadership Voices we need photos of our subject matter experts. Scroll through to see some of the portraits and schoolwork photography.

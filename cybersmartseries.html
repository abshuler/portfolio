Cybersmart Event Series. From concept to UI.

Overview

Client Carahsoft developed a multi-city roadshow in cities across the country to highlight leaders in state and local cybersecurity. The concept was developed in January 2019, with rolling development of individual events throughout the year.

Requirements

The overarching series name would be Cybersmart with a subject of cybersecurity. Each page would eventually need a landing page, but would be advertised on the home page as ‘coming soon’. The client wanted the series to be colorful and have elements specific to each city, however the subtitles (actual subject matter) of each event would not be decided until much later in the year. I developed two concepts for the client and laid out how they would be applied to each city throughout the year.

Option 1

Option 2

Client presented with two options, chose 1st option to move forward with. Events located in Atlanta, Austin, Los Angeles, Denver, and D.C.

Framework

I developed an overarching series lander showcasing the event series, and boldly announcing each event without any supporting information. I pulled in the closest font face commercially available at the time, montserrat, and built a framework for the inside of the individual pages based on sections given to me by the project manager.

Additional Work

Beyond the website, I created social media ads, a digital ad suite, and physical signage.

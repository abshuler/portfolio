CyberSmart Event Series

About

Carahsoft’s Cybersmart series is a 6-event roadshow that focuses on state and local cybersecurity adoption. With live events in Atlanta, Austin, LA, Denver, and DC throughout the course of a year, there needed to be an overarching theme in the series to generate long-lasting buzz.

My Role

Working with GEMG as their in-house designer for custom events, I was charged with designing the look and feel of the overarching event, look and feel of individual event, social materials, and physical event signage.

Option 1

Option 2

Client presented with two options, chose 1st option to move forward with. Events located in Atlanta, Austin, Los Angeles, Denver, and D.C.

Challenge

Each city had its own “subtitle” that was not finalized until 4-6 weeks before the event. I had to build a hub for all the pages without definitive locations, names, or information and continuously update it as new information came in. Knowing this, I made my design centric around the cities themselves while incorporating an overarching “cyber” theme. I presented the client with two distinct options and moved forward with the one they picked.

Deliverables

  • Two design options for overarching and individual event themes
  • 7 total distinct brand identities
  • A content hub for the series + individual event pages
  • Social ads and house ads to run on GEMG
  • Physical on-stage signage for each event

Insights

Oftentimes, we are asked to begin the design process without seeing the whole picture upfront. While I try to avoid this as much as I can, in some cases it is inevitable. In those scenarios, I work closely to communicate with my team and troubleshoot any problems that arrise meaning I am revising and refining constantly.

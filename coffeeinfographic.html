COFFEE INFOGRAPHIC
CHALLENGE
To create a type-based infographic about America's rapidly increasing coffee consumption.
MY ROLE
I took original photography, adjusted typography, and created a color pallette to match the coffee aesthetic. Obviously from my first drafts, I struggled with this assignment as I played around with what typography can and cannot accomplish. The second draft is what I turned in as an assignment, but later went back to redo this project.
DELIVERABLES
  • infographic optimized for print
INSIGHT
With other infographics I learned that every image should serve a purpose, and every piece of type should be as seamlessly integrated as possible. With this final version, I attempted to integrate as much of my added copy into the original photo I took to make it appear as one solid image.

About

Exploring AI in Government is a seven episode podcast series produced by Government Executive Media Group for Accenture starring government bigwigs like Congressman Will Hurd, Craig and OMB’s Suzette Kent. It focuses on the expansion of AI in government including automation, machine learning, and ethical implications.

My Role

Podcasts are a new product for GEMG and a lot of the job was researching and experimenting with best podcasting practices. Having worked with audio and video before, I tracked down the coordinator of our parent company’s Radio Studio, versed myself in Adobe Audition, and studied popular podcasts to understand the standards.

Challenge

The hardest part of a podcast is the coordination. With two hosts and two speakers, our team had to coordinate four packed schedules, sometimes meaning our guests were call-ins. We found ways around the poor audio of a phone call by using Skype’s internal record feature.

Fashion Design

About

Throughout college and in my spare time, I like to design and alter clothing. I competed in shows through school both for recycled designs and normal ready-to-wear fashion.

My Role

I find the strongest ways to continually foster creativity is to touch as many mediums as possible. Once upon a time I wanted to be a fashion designer; by entering in fashion shows, designing a ‘collection,’ recruiting friends as models, and sewing the designs myself I was able to live out that dream however temporarily.

Challenge

While attending school and living in dorms without space to cut and sew fabric, I made it work with a borrowed sewing machine and a dorm rec room.

Insights

If you’ve never heard the idiom ‘measure twice, cut once’ it is a seamstress’ best friend. During one show, I ran out of time and toilet paper rolls to finish a dress and had to improvise with tissue paper in order to get a design on stage. During another show, I physically sewed a model into the dress as I had cut it too tight to sew in a zipper. What may seem like a mistake to you is less visible on-stage. Sometimes, you have to be okay with imperfection.

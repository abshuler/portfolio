About

This site was coded using Dreamweaver in HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript with Bootstrap as a framework. You can find the raw code on Github.

Amelia’s personal branding is based off the notion of duality - much like sunflowers’ personas. They are bright and dark, strong and sturdy but still delicate and feminine, versatile in their purpose as a food and home adornment.

Utopia Std Bold is used as headlines, balanced by Futura PT Book as body copy. All icons were made / customized by Amelia using Illustrator.

Painting.

My love for design began when I was little with art classes. As I’ve grown older I’ve continued that passion even selling the canvases I’ve made.

As you’ll notice, the theme with sunflowers started with the paintings. I began by painting sunflowers one summer and my friends started to comission me to paint them to hang on their walls. It began a pattern that continues throughout my portfolio and even is blossoming into an Etsy shop.

Flatfit App

About

Flatfit, a start-up app based in Barcelona, helps pair roommates together for a better living environment. With just the CEO and COO as full-time employees, supporting and marketing web, Android, and iOS platforms can be a lot of work.

My Role

In a 6-month on-site internship, I worked heavily on social media marketing, app development, and even coded the front-end of their web app in HTML/CSS and Javascript. In the past year, the company has grown to ten cities in three continents.

Challenge

As being the only creative person on their small team, I had to wear a lot of hats - most notably conceptualizing features like “favorites” and “my listings,” and then mocking them for later implementation. In a brainstorming session, we conducted a user analysis which identified several use cases that were not previously planned for.

    Insights

    Beyond working at a start-up and the challenges that came with that, my main takeaway was the cultural differences between the US and Spain. In Spain, the main challenge is finding somewhere to live and there is less emphasis on finding a roommate. If you have limited options for rooms to live in, you take what you can get. In the US, the emphasis is finding someone to live with, as new housing complexes seem to pop-up every day. Even with knowing the language (prefieres a leer este pagina en espanol?

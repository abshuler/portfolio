Cybersmart Event Series. From concept to UI.

Overview

Client Carahsoft developed a multi-city roadshow across the united states to highlight leaders in state and local cybersecurity. The concept was developed in December 2018/January 2019, with rolling development of individual events throughout the year.

Interface Design

Social Marketing

Mision

The overarching series name would be Cybersmart with a subject of cybersecurity. The client knew the names of the cities, however the subtitles (actual subject matter) of each event, date of event, agenda, and speakers would not be decided until much later in the year.

Option 1

Option 2

Client presented with two options, chose 1st option to move forward with. Events located in Atlanta, Austin, Los Angeles, Denver, and Washington, D.C.

Vision

I wanted to create one overarching branding concept for the series that could easily be adapted when those subtitles came in. To differentiate the events early on with such little information, I decided to incorporate the event location into the design.

Strategy

Each page would eventually need a landing page, but would be advertised on the home page as ‘coming soon’. This attracted repeat users to the site and generated buzz about the events although they did not have much information. The bold colors worked together, but also stood as the sole differentiator before supporting information was added.

Design System: Carahsoft Cybersmart Series

November 2018 – December 2019

Overview

Background

Client Carahsoft developed a multi-city roadshow across the united states to highlight leaders in state and local cybersecurity. The concept was developed in December 2018/January 2019, with rolling development of individual events throughout the year.

Challenge

The overarching series name would be ‘Cybersmart’ with each event covering a different subject within cybersecurity. The client knew the names of the cities, however the subtitles (actual subject matter) of each event, date of event, agenda, and speakers would not be decided until much later in the year.

My Role

The project manager and I decided it would be easiest to create one overarching branding concept for the series that could easily be adapted when those subtitles came in.

Look and Feel

Mood Board

I created a mood board for internal use pulling together different concept ideas I thought were cool. I knew the client liked the previous event series they sponsored with us, so I picked bright colors to mimic that design.

Concept A & B Development

I knew the name of the series “Cybersmart” and the names of the cities. Beyond that, there was little else at this point differentiating the events (they were a year away). I created two overarching series logos that conveyed cybersmart, and used the skylines and street maps of the individual cities to differentiate them. Each city would have a color or series of colors assigned to them to differentiate the events from one another.

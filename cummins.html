CUMMINS FIRE POWER WEBSITE
CHALLENGE
The last update to the Cummins Fire Power Website was evidently in the 1990s and it was in desperate need of an overhaul. Because Cummins, Inc., a parent company who bought the Cummins Fire Power Franchise, had a pre-existing website, the objective was to incorporate the Fire Power Site into the larger Cummins Site.
MY ROLE
I worked closely with engineers, technical writers, and management to reorganize and consolidate the navigation, create original content for the site, add a customizable marketing page specific to Fire Power Engines, and finished by making them a mock-up design in a software they were familiar with - Excel.
DELIVERABLES
  • redesign of site map
  • mock-up in excel
  • original marketing photos and copy
INSIGHT
This project forced me to be extremely creative. I had to provide for a company branch with no marketing-focused employee, an easy-to-understand, editable outline of their new site. Additionally, I had to understand the product, a highly technical and specific engine type, enough to be able to organize the information for customers and sell it to them.
